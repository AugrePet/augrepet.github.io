    • The humble beginnings of AugrePet

    AugrePet is short for Augmented Reality Pet.
    We believe in taking our love for caring for critters to the next evolutionary step:
    Augmented Reality

    We first came about this idea when one of our team, Herald, wanted to get a pet. However, due to a myriad of reasons that we will not cover here, Herald was unable to get a real-life pet for himself. Looking at the current state of pet applications and devices made him despair, so he roped us together and formed this team to begin work on AugrePet.
